ABOUT US

Plant Life is an app that was made specifically to help people find the best plants to grow based on their personal preferences and requirements. You can enter your own settings to find the perfect plant to grow, as well as get some tips and interesting/helpful ideas for growing your plants. To begin, please head to the Choose Plant tab on the top bar.
