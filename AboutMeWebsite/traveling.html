Real World Connections

Traveling is a great way to learn about different nationalities and immerse yourself in different cultures. Schools often encourage traveling by offering students trips to different countries. Universities encourage this also through study abroad programs. To add on, many schools offer student exchange programs, where students can study and live with a host family of a particular country. There are countless benefits to traveling.
